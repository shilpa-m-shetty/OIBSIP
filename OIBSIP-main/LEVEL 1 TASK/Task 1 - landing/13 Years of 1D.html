    Karnataka Ratna

    Dr.Puneeth Rajkumar

Welcome to the official Karnataka Ratna Dr.Puneeth Rajkuma website.
Puneeth Rajkumar was a celebrated Indian actor, playback singer, and television presenter,
primarily known for his work in Kannada cinema.He began his acting career as a child artist,
winning the National Film Award for Best Child Artist for his role in "Bettada Hoovu."
Puneeth's successful transition to lead roles established him as a superstar, with
numerous blockbuster films like "Appu," "Milana," and "Raajakumara" to his credit.
Beyond acting, he was a talented singer and popular TV host. His contributions
to the entertainment industry earned him numerous awards and a devoted fan
following, solidifying his legacy in Indian cinema.
